About myGenre

At the heart of our platform lies a genre classification algorithm that analyzes the intricate patterns and nuances of each song you submit. Using advanced techniques and machine learning, our algorithm dives deep into the audio features of the song, extracting key characteristics that define its genre.

How It Works

Song Submission:

Simply upload your favorite song to our platform. Whether it's a classic hit or a hidden gem, our algorithm is ready to decode its genre (or at least try).

Genre Classification:

Our algorithm processes the audio input, focusing on elements like rhythm, melody, and harmonics. Through careful analysis, it identifies the genre that best encapsulates the essence of the song.

Immediate Results:

Within moments, you'll receive the classification results. Discover the diverse world of music genres, from rock and pop to jazz, disco, hiphop and many more.
